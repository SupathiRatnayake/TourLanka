Polonnaruwa City

Polonnaruwa is situated in North Central Province in Srilanka. Polonnaruwa City has been built as two main areas. One of these is Kaduruwela city and other one is royal ancient city of the Kingdom of Polonnaruwa. Polonnaruwa city belongs 3,293 km² area from whole area of Sri Lanka.
