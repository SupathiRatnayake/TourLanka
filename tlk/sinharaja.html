Sinharaja rainforest


Sinharaja Rainforest is the first world heritage forest in Sri Lanka. It is a very beautiful green forest. This forest is a rich combination of vegetation, animals, birds, insects and reptiles. Sinharaja lies between 300 metres and 1000 metres above the sea level. It is about 9000 hectares in extent.

Sinharaja rainforest has many tall trees suth as “Diyana”, “Navanda”, “Mihiriya” and “Beraliya”. The hight of these trees are varies between 35 to 40 metres. They are the endemic trees in Sri Lanka. We can see many creepers in the forest.some of them are Venivel, Puswel and Vevel. There are some plants that grow on trees such as orchids and Benduru. Ferns and mosses grow well in Sinharaja rainforest because of the heavy rainfall.
